Hello world! This is the Travelr Bot V3 home page :)

Travelr takes your current location and destination and provides routes of transportation

We currently support Uber, Lyft, and Transit for all cities that support those services.

Terms of use

Travelr is not responsible for bad directions and or mis-guided routes. This is a free service in alpha. The bot is free to use for the single purpose of transportation within a city. Any use beyond that of transportation inquiries will result in useless information. Please let us know of any bugs or issues and will will try to help. We do not take or keep any information expect for the locations used during the dialog.

Privacy Statement

This bot is in current alpha. We do not retain any information. Any and all locations are not retained.

Your information will not be saved, sold, used, or transmitted to any other entity. Your locations are only used for within the bot service.<./p>
